Course Notes

Chapter Main Contents
Chapter 2 Numerical Analysis: Fixed point methods, Newton-Rhapson
Chapter 3 Optimization: Unconstrained optimization, constrained optimization, daulity.
Chapter 4 Regression: Least squares, Levenberg-Marquardt, Bayesian regression
Chapter 5 Ordinary Differential Equations
Chapter 6 Numerical integration and differentiation
Chapter 7 Dimensionality Reduction
Chapter 9 Classification: K Nearest Neighbors, Naive Bayes, AdaBoost, Support Vector Machines, Gaussian Process Classification, Tree Methods, Mixture Models
Chapter 10 Neural networks: Hebbian Learning, Hopfield Networks, Perceptrons, Backpropogation, Self organizing map, Convolutional Networks
